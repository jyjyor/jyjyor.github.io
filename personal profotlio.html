About

Art is how I pay attention to the world

I'm Jiayi — an artist and photographer based in Los Angeles, currently studying at Santa Monica College pursuing degrees in Arts and Communication. My work lives in the space between what people notice and what they overlook.

From documenting discarded objects on city sidewalks to working with children through art, I believe creativity is how we make sense of what surrounds us.
